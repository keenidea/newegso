ABOUT US

EGSO Company Limited is an Engineering company registered under the companies Act, 1963, Act 176 of the law of Ghana. The company was incorporated in the 22nd day of June, 2016 and commence to business in the 24th day of June, 2016. The company has executed numerous projects mainly in the western region and other part of the country.

EGSO Company Limited is currently operating in Land Surveying, Manufacturing of Electrical and Electronic Devices, Quantity Surveying, Building and Civil Works, Structural Design, Architectural Design, Road Constructions, Valuation and Information Technology services. We bring innovative ideas into reality.

We have a wide range of clients, include the government of Ghana, individuals, churches and cooperate bodies and district assemblies.

OUR DEPARTMENTS

LAND SURVEY

This department under EGSO COMPANY LIMITED is solely into land documentations and engineering surveying. The department makes sure that reliable and accurate documents relating to land registrations are provided to its clients. It also makes sure that, settlement layouts are well planned and demarcated on grounds to make movement very easy within residential areas.

BUILDING AND CIVIL WORKS

The department controls any work pertaining to full structural design, architectural design, building construction, road constructions, quantity survey and valuation.

MANUFACTURING

The manufacturing department does only manufacturing and assembling of electrical and electric products. They invent and design any specs of device no matter how it is, it is made internally here in Ghana. The department designs and manufacture devices such as smart home device, fuel analyzer, high level communication system for military, security systems and many more.

INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

PRINTING PRESS

the printing press department does everything concerning printing. It is into general printing and secretariat services. It does printings such as, banners, books, T-Shirt, complementary cards. For the secretariat aspect, it does general typing, printing of documents (A4 and A3), photocopying, scanning, binding and lamination.

MARKETING AND SALES

The marketing and Sales department is into marketing the services of the company to the people. They see to it that, the company gets contracts and also gather information from the people concerning our services

PROCUREMENT

The procurement department is concerned with the purchasing of any product or device for the company. It makes sure that good and profitable products are bought for the company at an accepted and reasonable cost

ADMINISTRATION

This department administers all activities carried out in the company. It does supervision of the various departments to make sure that every departmental body performs it duties daily. It collects necessary information from the various departments and report to the Board if necessary to be addressed.

HEALTH AND SAFETY

The health and safety department ensures that the safety, health and welfare of all employees are fully protected. It is designed to comply with the standards of the occupational Safety and Health Administration, and to a safe and injury/illness free workers.
